# Beyond-TOC Notes

These concepts are not always listed in beginner course outlines, but they improve training quality when woven into Day 1 delivery.

## Reproducibility

Learners should understand that notebooks should run again and produce similar results.

Teaching line: "Good analysis should be repeatable."

## Raw vs Cleaned Data

Never overwrite raw data too early.

Teaching line: "Raw data is the source of truth. Cleaned data is our working version."

## Evidence-Based Observations

Students should not write unsupported opinions.

Teaching line: "Every observation should point to a number, chart, or table."

## Small Dataset Caution

Teaching line: "This small dataset is for learning. Real business conclusions need more data and validation."

## Ethical Handling of Data

Teaching line: "Use only data you are allowed to use. Avoid exposing private information."
